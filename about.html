About me and my goals

I am a visual artist and aspiring web developer. My creative background allows me to approach design projects from a different angle, with a strong focus on making the user experience both beautiful and intuitive. I understand the importance of colors, composition, and storytelling from both an artistic and technical perspective. As I continue to develop my knowledge, I look forward to collaborating on projects where I can apply my creativity, and problem solving skills. Download C.V

About my art

Over the last decade I have been practicing as a contemporary artist, primarily working on abstract collages and textile banners. I have staged several solo exhibitions under ultra-violet light, with which I love to entice my audience! My influences include tattoo culture, graffiti and folk art which I blend with more serious interests in technology and philosophy. The results are fun bright, bold and busy images, with an edifying complexity to their underlying themes. As well as fine art I often work on illustration, and also spent a period of time creating surface patterns for my own alternative fashion label. View my art
